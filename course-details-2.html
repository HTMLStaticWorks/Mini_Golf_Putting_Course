Pirate Cove Challenge

Discover holes 1-18, learn official obstacle mechanics, and check the current course leaderboard records.

The Blueprint

Walk the Plank & Master the Slopes

The Pirate Cove Challenge combines storytelling with skill. Each hole introduces a narrative step, leading you from the sandy shores of Port Royal to the interior captain's cavern where the final putt awaits.

This course features steep ramps, tunnels beneath replica cannons, and water streams that can carry your ball away if your putting speed is too fast.

Difficulty Rating

Moderate (Perfect for kids 8+ and adults)

Avg. Playtime

45 to 55 minutes for a group of four

Official Rules of Play

  • 6-Stroke Limit: Maximum of 6 strokes per hole. If you don't sink the ball, record a score of 7 and proceed.
  • Out of Bounds: If the ball leaves the green, replace it at the exit point with a 1-stroke penalty.
  • Obstacle Courtesy: Do not stand on or walk across the raised obstacle bridges and ship structures.
  • Safety First: Keep putter head below knee level at all times during swings.
Obstacle Highlights

Inside the Challenges

Hole 7: Loop-the-Loop

Strike the ball with clean velocity to force it up and around the loop. Too soft, and it rolls backward; too hard, and it skips the green.

Hole 12: Pirate Windmill

Time your stroke perfectly to roll the ball between the rotating wooden sails. Getting through guarantees a clear shot at a hole-in-one.

Hole 15: Cannons & Cascades

A narrow bridge spans across a running water creek. A steady putter hand is required to keep the ball on course without dropping into the hazard.
