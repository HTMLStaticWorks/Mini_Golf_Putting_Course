Tropical Oasis

Explore our family-favorite garden layout, custom streams, and relaxing floral landscapes.

The Garden Walk

Relaxing Pathways & Gentle Waterways

The Tropical Oasis course is designed for players of all ages and skills. Surrounded by towering palms, blooming tropical hibiscuses, and gentle streams, this course offers a peaceful, scenic backdrop for a relaxed game.

Features include lightweight turf slopes, scenic garden bridges, and shallow streams that border the holes, making it fun for toddlers, casual date nights, and beginners alike.

Difficulty Rating

Easy (Ideal for kids, families, and beginners)

Avg. Playtime

30 to 40 minutes for a group of four

Official Rules of Play

  • 6-Stroke Limit: Maximum of 6 strokes per hole. If you don't sink the ball, record a score of 7 and proceed.
  • Out of Bounds: If the ball leaves the green, replace it at the exit point with a 1-stroke penalty.
  • Flowerbed Courtesy: Please keep feet on the artificial turf and do not step into flowerbeds.
  • Safety First: Keep putter head below knee level at all times during swings.
Obstacle Highlights

Inside the Gardens

Hole 3: Gentle Stream

Navigate a narrow turf pathway flanked on both sides by clear running water. Keep your putter face square for a straight roll across the bridge.

Hole 8: Floral Maze

Find the optimal putting lane through green garden hedges and blooming hibiscuses. A light stroke allows the slope to carry the ball to the cup.

Hole 14: Oasis Waterfall

Putt behind a beautiful cascading rock waterfall structure. A steady speed is critical to avoid the water mist and curve into the cup.
